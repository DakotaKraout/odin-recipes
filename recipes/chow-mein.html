Chow Mein

Description

Chow mein is a Chinese dish made from stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu.

Ingredients

  • Stir-fried noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Tofu

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add chopped vegetables.
  3. Stir and saute
  4. Add water
  5. Add stir-fried noodles
  6. Serve chow mein steaming hot in serving bowls.
