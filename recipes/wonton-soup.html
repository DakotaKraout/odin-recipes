Wonton Soup

Description

Wonton is Chinese dish similar to Dumplings made with flour and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Vegetables
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare a bowl.
  2. Place filling ingredients in the bowl.
  3. Wrap wontons.
  4. Add them in bowl.
  5. Add boiled water in bowl.
  6. Serve Wonton Soup.
